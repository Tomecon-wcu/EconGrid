📚 Concept: When milk supply increases (S1 → S2), at the original price ($2.95) there's excess supply (surplus). Farmers compete by lowering prices, moving down the demand curve. Lower prices stimulate quantity demanded while reducing quantity suppliers are willing to provide. The market clears at the new E2 equilibrium.

How to use: Drag the "Supply Increase" slider right. Watch S1 shift to S2, the surplus area appear, and the equilibrium move from E1 to E2.
