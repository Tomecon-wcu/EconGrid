Market Adjustment Story

  • 📉 Decrease in Supply — Supply curve shifts left (S → S₂). At every price, less is offered.
  • ⚠️ Shortage Emerges — At the original price of $3.00, quantity demanded exceeds quantity supplied, creating a shortage.
  • 🔺 Price Rises — The shortage puts upward pressure on price as buyers compete for scarce bars.
  • 🔻 Qty Demanded Falls — Higher price causes consumers to buy fewer bars, moving up the demand curve.
