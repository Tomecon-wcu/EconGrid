Market Adjustment Process

When Demand ↑

  1. Shortage emerges (Qty Demanded > Qty Supplied)
  2. Prices rise to clear the market
  3. Quantity supplied increases
  4. Quantity demanded falls
  5. New equilibrium reached

When Supply ↓

  1. Shortage emerges (Qty Demanded > Qty Supplied)
  2. Prices rise to clear the market
  3. Quantity demanded falls
  4. New equilibrium at lower quantity
  5. Equilibrium price higher

When Supply ↑

  1. Surplus emerges (Qty Supplied > Qty Demanded)
  2. Prices fall to clear the market
  3. Quantity demanded increases
  4. New equilibrium at higher quantity
  5. Equilibrium price lower
