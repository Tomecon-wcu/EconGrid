Supply & Demand Graph — Milk

1

Initial Equilibrium: Price = $2.50, Quantity = 100M gal. Supply (S) and Demand (D) intersect.

2

Supply Decreases (S → S₁): The supply curve shifts left. At the old price, suppliers offer less milk.

3

Shortage Emerges: Qty demanded > Qty supplied at $2.50 → upward pressure on price.

4

New Equilibrium: Price rises, Qty demanded falls, Qty supplied rises until market clears.
