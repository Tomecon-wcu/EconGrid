What's Happening?

Move the slider to shift the demand curve rightward, simulating an increase in demand (e.g., rising income, changing preferences). At the original price of $8, a shortage emerges. Rising prices then incentivize producers to supply more, while consumers demand less — moving the market to a new equilibrium.
