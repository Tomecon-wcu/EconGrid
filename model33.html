📈 What's Happening?

The market is at its initial equilibrium: price = $5.99/lb and quantity = 250,000 lbs. This is where the supply curve (S) and demand curve (D₁) intersect. Drag the slider to the right to simulate an increase in demand for beef — such as a health trend, population growth, or a rise in consumer income.
