Adjustment Sequence

  1. 1 D₁ → D₂ — Demand shifts right. At every price, more fish is wanted.
  2. 2 Shortage at P₀ = $1.50 — QD now exceeds QS. Shelves empty faster than they're stocked.
  3. 3 Price rises — Buyers bid price up; sellers respond by bringing more supply to market.
  4. 4 New E₂ reached — Higher P clears the market. Both QS and QD are now higher than Q₁.
