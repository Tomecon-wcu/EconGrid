How to Read This

Demand (D) slopes downward — as price rises, buyers want less honey.

Supply (S) slopes upward — as price rises, beekeepers offer more honey.

Equilibrium ● is where the curves intersect — the market-clearing price and quantity.

Shortage: Qd > Qs → price rises → Qd↓, Qs↑ → new equilibrium.

Surplus: Qs > Qd → price falls → Qd↑, Qs↓ → new equilibrium.
