Market Adjustment Process

  1. Demand or supply shifts — curve moves left or right.
  2. At old price, a shortage (demand > supply) or surplus (supply > demand) appears.
  3. Price adjusts upward (shortage) or downward (surplus).
  4. Higher price → QS rises, QD falls → new equilibrium reached.
