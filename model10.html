Step 1 of 5
Original equilibrium: P₁ = $1.68/lb, Q₁ = 13.0 bn lbs
Supply Curve (S) Upward-sloping. U.S. cheese producers supply more at higher prices.
Original Demand (D₁) — 2023 At P₁=$1.68/lb, consumers demand Q₁=13.0 bn lbs/yr.
New Demand (D₂) — 2024 Demand rises due to specialty/artisan cheese growth & strong exports. D₁ shifts rightward to D₂.
Shortage at P₁ At the old price, quantity demanded (14.65 bn) exceeds quantity supplied (13.0 bn), creating a shortage of ~1.65 bn lbs.
Market Adjustment (Navy arrows) Shortage drives prices up. Qs rises along S; Qd falls along D₂ until E₂: P₂=$1.83/lb, Q₂=13.7 bn lbs.
